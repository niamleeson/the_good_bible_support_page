Frequently Asked Questions

Which Bible translations are available?

The Good Bible currently includes KJV and WEB in English. More translations and language support are coming soon.

How do I highlight text?

Switch to Highlight mode using the floating toolbar, then long-press and drag over the words you want to highlight. You can choose from 12 colors and organize highlights into groups.

Can I take notes on specific verses?

Yes. Select a verse or word, then create a note anchored to that location. Notes support rich text formatting including bold, italic, lists, and more. Organize them with folders and tags.

How do I create a reading plan?

Go to Reading Plans and tap the + button. Choose your start and end dates, select which books to include, and the app will generate a daily reading schedule for you.

Can I draw on the Bible pages?

Yes. Switch to Draw mode using the toolbar. You can sketch with pressure-sensitive strokes, choose colors and widths, and your drawings are saved per chapter.

How do I change the font or colors?

Open Settings to customize fonts, font sizes, line height, letter spacing, page colors, verse colors, and more. Each language can have its own font settings.

Is my data stored locally?

Yes. All your notes, highlights, drawings, and reading progress are stored locally on your device.

Privacy Policy

The Good Bible does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data including notes, highlights, and reading progress is stored locally on your device. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
