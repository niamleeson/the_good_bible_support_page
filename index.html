Frequently Asked Questions

Which Bible translations are available?

The Good Bible currently includes KJV and WEB in English. More translations and language support are coming soon.

Is this app free?

Yes. The Good Bible is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Is my data stored locally?

Yes. All your notes, highlights, drawings, and reading progress are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at thegoodbiblehelp@gmail.com with a description of what happened and we'll look into it.

How to Use The Good Bible

Reading

  • Scroll continuously through chapters with smooth infinite scrolling.
  • Switch between translations (KJV, WEB) instantly from the menu.
  • Jump to any verse using the Go-to-Verse dialog — type references like "John 3:16" or "Rom 12".
  • Navigate books from the book grid with customizable layouts.
  • Read in single or multi-column mode.

Toolbar & Interaction Modes

  • Tap the floating toolbar to switch between modes: Read, Select, Highlight, Draw, and Note.
  • The toolbar can be positioned on the left or right side of the screen.
  • Each mode changes how your taps and gestures interact with the text.

Highlighting

  • Switch to Highlight mode, then long-press and drag over words to highlight them.
  • Choose from 12 color swatches.
  • Organize highlights into named groups with custom colors.
  • Use the eraser tool to remove highlights by dragging over them.
  • Undo and redo highlight actions from the toolbar.

Notes

  • Create rich text notes with bold, italic, underline, lists, blockquotes, headings, and more.
  • Anchor notes to specific words, verses, or verse ranges — they appear inline with the text.
  • Tap an inline note card to expand and read it. Tap the edit button to open the full editor.
  • Organize notes with folders and tags.
  • Search across all your notes instantly with full-text search.

Drawing & Annotations

  • Switch to Draw mode to sketch directly on Bible pages.
  • Pressure-sensitive strokes that respond to your stylus or finger pressure.
  • Choose from multiple pen colors and adjust stroke width.
  • Toggle left and right drawing margins for extra space.
  • Use lasso select to move drawings around the page.
  • Undo and redo drawing strokes.
  • Drawings are saved per chapter and persist across sessions.

Select & Copy

  • Switch to Select mode and tap verses to select them.
  • Long-press and drag to select individual words.
  • Copy selected text to clipboard with a customizable template.
  • Choose whether to include verse numbers and how they are formatted.
  • Option to place each verse on a new line when copying.

Reading Plans

  • Create custom reading plans with your own start and end dates.
  • Choose which books to include — the whole Bible or a specific range.
  • The app generates a daily reading schedule automatically.
  • Track your progress with a calendar view showing completed days.
  • See daily reading summaries with chapter and word counts.

Search

  • Full-text search across all verses in your current translation.
  • Search your notes by title or content.
  • Browse topics alphabetically or search by keyword.
  • Results appear instantly as you type.

Customization

  • Customize fonts, font sizes, font weights, line height, and letter spacing.
  • Set independent font settings per language.
  • Adjust page, verse, menu, and title colors independently.
  • Configure margins (uniform or per-side).
  • Customize verse number appearance: size, color, and vertical offset.
  • Rename books with custom display names.
  • Assign custom colors to books in the book grid.
  • Live preview pane shows changes as you adjust settings.

Navigation History

  • Your reading history is tracked automatically with timestamps.
  • Jump back to any previously visited location.
  • Your last reading position is saved and restored when you reopen the app.

Two-Finger Pan

  • In any mode, use two fingers to scroll the page — even while in Draw or Highlight mode.
  • Two-finger panning has the same smooth momentum scrolling as normal reading.

Privacy Policy

Overview

The Good Bible is a Bible reading application designed to work entirely offline. Your privacy is important to us, and we have built the app with that principle in mind.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics services, and does not include any third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. This includes:

  • Your reading position and preferences (font, colors, layout)
  • Highlights and highlight groups you create
  • Notes and drawings you create
  • Reading plan progress
  • Bible text databases used for offline reading

This data is only accessible to the app and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection to function. No data is sent to or received from any server during normal use.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, crash reporters, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
